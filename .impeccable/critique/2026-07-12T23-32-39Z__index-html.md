## Overall Impression

Real photography and a genuine per-content-line color system give this page a POV that keeps it out of slop territory. But a confirmed glassmorphism violation, a real WCAG contrast failure on the hero tagline, and a broken-promise nav card ("En Vivo" → historia) are concrete, fixable cracks in an otherwise solid foundation.

## What's Working

1. CTA strategy matches the actual product goal — the `.social-strip` pill (real primary CTA per PRODUCT.md) sits under the hero and auto-hides on scroll instead of nagging.
2. Pure-CSS hero crossfade: 8 real local photos, 48s loop, `prefers-reduced-motion` respected.
3. Per-content-line color system (Fotos/Cultura/Historia/En Vivo) implemented as real CSS custom properties, not just varied photos.
